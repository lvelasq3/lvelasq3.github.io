Engineering research has the greatest impact when it is shared broadly. Through mentorship, public engagement, and hands-on demonstrations, I work to make prosthetics, robotics, and human-centered engineering more accessible to students, families, and the broader community.

---

# Mentorship

<div style="display:grid; grid-template-columns:repeat(auto-fit,minmax(300px,1fr)); gap:2rem; margin-bottom:4rem;">

<div>
<img src="/images/vtsi_outreach.png" alt="VTSI Mentorship" style="width:100%; border-radius:12px;">

<h2>Vivien Thomas Scholars Initiative (VTSI)</h2>

<p>
Through the Vivien Thomas Scholars Initiative, I mentor undergraduate researchers pursuing independent research experiences in engineering and biomedical science. Mentorship focuses on technical skill development, scientific communication, and preparation for graduate study and research careers.
</p>
</div>

<div>
<img src="/images/wise_outreach.png" alt="WISE Mentorship" style="width:100%; border-radius:12px;">

<h2>WISE Research Mentorship</h2>

<p>
As part of the Whiting School of Engineering's WISE programs, I mentor high-school students participating in laboratory research experiences. Students gain exposure to prosthetics research, engineering design, experimental methods, and scientific problem solving.
</p>
</div>

<div>
<img src="/images/reu_outreach.png" alt="NSF REU Mentorship" style="width:100%; border-radius:12px;">

<h2>NSF REU Programs</h2>

<p>
I support undergraduate researchers participating in National Science Foundation Research Experiences for Undergraduates (REU) programs through project supervision, technical training, and research mentoring.
</p>
</div>

</div>

# Community Outreach

<div style="display:grid; grid-template-columns:repeat(auto-fit,minmax(300px,1fr)); gap:2rem;">

<div>
<img src="/images/msc_outreach.png" alt="Maryland Science Center" style="width:100%; border-radius:12px;">

<h2>Maryland Science Center</h2>

<p>
I help develop and present interactive exhibits that introduce visitors to prosthetic technology, robotics, and human-centered engineering. These demonstrations provide hands-on opportunities for audiences of all ages to engage with current research.
</p>
</div>

<div>
<img src="/images/campbmore_outreach.png" alt="Camp BMore" style="width:100%; border-radius:12px;">

<h2>Camp BMore</h2>

<p>
Through Camp BMore, our team designs and fabricates customized prosthetic devices for children and families affected by cancer. This initiative combines engineering design with direct community impact and patient-centered problem solving.
</p>
</div>

<div>
<img src="/images/barclay_outreach.png" alt="Barclay School Tours" style="width:100%; border-radius:12px;">

<h2>Barclay School STEM Tours</h2>

<p>
In partnership with the Whiting Institute for Science Education (WISE) outreach initiatives, I host laboratory tours and engineering demonstrations for local middle-school students, introducing them to prosthetics, robotics, and pathways into STEM careers.
</p>
</div>

</div>
